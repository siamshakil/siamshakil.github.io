§ 01 - About

Graduated as a mechanical engineer who crosses into commercial territory - not because I drifted, but because the most interesting problems live at the boundary between how something works and whether it should exist at all.

At {{ site.author.org }} I work on Design R&D and technical business development, applying structured methods - TRIZ, QFD, Pugh matrix - to problems that most treat as purely intuitive. I hold a {{ site.author.cert }} credential and work daily in SolidWorks and ANSYS.

My current technical interest is body-in-white design for local vehicle manufacturing in Bangladesh, where imported drivetrains meet local packaging constraints. The EV transition in South Asia is early-stage and underspecified - exactly where engineering thinking compounds fastest.
