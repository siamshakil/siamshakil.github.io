![Samaras Suppliers drop box](/assets/images/projects/samaras-suppliers.png)

The model is named **Samaras Suppliers** after the samara seed: like a maple seed, it has wings, and
while free-falling it rotates - which slows the descent and helps it land safely, decreasing the impact
velocity.

## How it protects the payload

- The attached propeller rotates during landing, decreasing the landing impact.
- The front spherical object absorbs much of the force and translates the stress wave through the
  meshed, optimised shell.
- An extra container is included to act as an insulator, keeping the chamber at the optimal temperature
  for drugs or chemicals.
- A hook is attached so the container can be dropped with a parachute from great height.

The whole model is built around that same samara principle.

## Optimisation

One of the parts is optimised using a **generative design** technique in **Solid Edge**. After generating
roughly a **50% reduction in mass**, the overall part was simulated for a **48 lb load** (including dead
weight).

## What the attachment can carry

- Delicate instruments
- Blood
- Reagents
- Vaccines
- Operating instruments
- Seeds
- Fertilizer
