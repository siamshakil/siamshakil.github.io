Research and concept development for body-in-white monocoque chassis design, adapted specifically
to the constraints of Bangladesh's emerging local vehicle manufacturing landscape.

## The core constraint

Bangladesh's local manufacturing model imports drivetrains and builds packaging around them —
the inverse of how most automakers design. This shifts the engineering challenge from powertrain
integration to **packaging optimization within a fixed drivetrain envelope**.

## Scope of work

- BIW fundamentals and monocoque chassis development methodology
- Occupant packaging analysis within imported-drivetrain constraints
- CATIA V5-based learning roadmap and workflow setup
- Market analysis across Toyota, Nissan, and Honda pricing in BDT — reconditioned vs.
  brand-new segment dynamics, to ground engineering decisions in commercial reality

## Status

Ongoing self-directed research, building toward applied domain expertise for local
manufacturing applications.
