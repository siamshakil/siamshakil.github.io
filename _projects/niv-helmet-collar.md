This is a project done jointly with **Team Durbar Kandari**, based in Bangladesh.

The main objective was to optimise the collar of the **NIV (non-invasive ventilation) helmet** to:

- prevent leaks,
- make it easy to assemble, and
- keep it manufacturable on popular 3D printers.

The final design was **3D printed and tested for leaks**.
