Full SolidWorks design of a spring-balanced parallelogram linkage desk lamp, inspired by the
neutral-equilibrium comparison between Anglepoise and Tolomeo designs.

## Design brief

The goal: a 600mm reach articulated arm that holds any position without locking mechanisms,
using only spring-balanced moment equilibrium - the same principle that makes a Tolomeo lamp
feel weightless in the hand.

## Process

- Moment equilibrium analysis across the full range of motion
- Parallelogram linkage design ensuring the lamp head stays level at every arm angle
- Spring rate calculation for Al 6061-T6 construction
- Motion Analysis validation in SolidWorks to confirm neutral equilibrium behavior
- FEA setup targeting FOS ≥ 2.0 under worst-case loading

## Outcome

A working CAD model validated against hand calculations, with FEA confirming structural margin
at the extended position - the highest-stress configuration for this linkage type.
