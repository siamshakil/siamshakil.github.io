Technical business development research for Akij Renewable Energy and Automobiles Ltd. (AREAL),
covering the BAW brand EV lineup and clean energy division strategy in the Bangladesh market.

## Why this matters

EV market entry in Bangladesh isn't a marketing problem — it's a technical-commercial alignment
problem. Vehicle specifications, charging infrastructure, and C&I account positioning all have
to move together for an entry strategy to work.

## Areas covered

- BAW brand partnership structure and recent vehicle launches
- Competitive landscape across the BD clean energy / EV space
- C&I (Commercial & Industrial) account positioning strategy
- Commercial feasibility framing grounded in technical specification realities

## Approach

Combining engineering literacy with business development methodology — reading a spec sheet
and a procurement process with equal fluency.
